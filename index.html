Tokyo, Japan (mobile)

Regardless of where you’re from, Japan boasts a variety of wonders, many of which are found in Tokyo, one of their maintourist destinations.

Whether it's ramen, anime, high-end streetwear, or crazy car culture, Tokyo has never-ending things to explore. Tokyo isn’t just a normal city, rather it's the largest city, by population, in the world and one of the largest by urban land area.

There are truly countless things that make Tokyo speciale: the RHD rule, the public vending machines, the lack of trash cans, anime, their unique history—-however, the funny thing is tha that you’ll most likely figure out what's really speciale along the way of your visit. That's what makes it special.
